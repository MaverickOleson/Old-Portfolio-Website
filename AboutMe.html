About my personality

Hello there! Came to see what my personality is like, eh? Well, I appreciate that. I'd say my personality is quite peculiar from others, for better and, occasionally, for worse. For starters, I can get very absorbed into practically anything I'm doing, especially projects. I can go long hours just working on one thing, whether it be for myself or external work. However, if the project is more of a creative project, usually something I don't fully have conceptualized ahead of time, I can get stuck on one small detail for minutes... minutes that add up and cost me a lot more time than it would have if I had decided to not focus on that detail. But this can lead to some really fantastic creations, in my opinion. Plus, this habit does not become an issue when I'm given a deadline. I'm often able to assess the amount of time I'm able to work on something well enough to get things done in the amount of time I have. The only trouble I have with this sort of time management comes when my schedule is interrupted by unforseen forces, beyond my control. If you're curious as to why I get so focused on the details of a creative project, it's because I'm naturally not satisfied with giving something with as little effort as possible, if I can help it. When I have the opportunity to create something, I have to, in the least, make it foolproof, and, if it's something I'm very interested in or that is important, I'll usually take steps to go above and beyond what I normally would. To carry on, another aspect of my personality is that I love to be productive. I have to be doing something worthwhile, most of the time, if I'm able to be doing anything of my own, although I occassionally spend downtime. When I do anything productive, though, it's preferable that I get to do something that matters to me, but, when I do get to work for someone else, it'll become something worth doing that I'll give me best in. A final fact about my personality is that I have a large imagination. I often daydream briefly throughout my day, and I love being creative.
